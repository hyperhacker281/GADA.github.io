About Us

Gada Electronics also gives its customers multiple payment gateways so that they can choose to pay via their preferred payment option. A customer could choose from any payment mode like credit or debit cards, payment wallets and cash-on-delivery. Gada Electronics also allows its customers to avail of various Easy EMI options on cards and through consumer finance. Additionally, to ensure that customers enjoy their product to the fullest, Gada Electronics encourages purchase of Extended Warranty, an insurance that safeguards their product against mishaps.
In line with what Gada Electronics stands for, it also offers its customers an Assured Lifetime Service, an assurance of exceptional after sales service – which can be simply availed by walking into the nearest Gada Electronics store or calling up the 24x7 customer care centre at 7207-666-000


With this intent to delight customers all along, Gada Electronics launched its own label products in 2008. Brand Gada Electronics stands for – Life More Beautiful; the promise is based not just on the beauty in the way they look but also the value they offer to a customer’s life. The range is a perfect fit, for those who desire a completely feature-packed and quality product.
Gada Electronics is a brand of Infiniti Retail Ltd., which is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group.
